Crystal Collector Game



You will be given a random number at the start of the gamel.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

Each the game starts the values of each crystal will be randomized.
